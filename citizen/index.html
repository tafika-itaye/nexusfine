How NexusFine Works

01

📋 Fine Issued

A traffic officer issues your fine electronically in the field. You receive an SMS notification instantly with your reference number.

02

🔍 Look Up Online

Enter your plate or reference number here. View full fine details including offence, location, officer, and amount due.

03

💳 Pay Instantly

Choose Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, bank transfer, or card. Payment confirmation is immediate with no manual steps.

04

📄 Get Receipt

Download or print your official payment receipt as PDF. Your record is updated in real time on the MPS system.
